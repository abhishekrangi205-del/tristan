# Tristan Ritchie - Theme & Homepage Proposals

This document outlines proposed design changes for Tristan Ritchie's rebranding, focusing on a new visual theme and ideas for integrating his headshot into the homepage. **These are proposals only and have not yet been implemented.**

## 1. New Theme Concepts

To complement Tristan's professional image (particularly the royal blue suit in his headshot), we propose moving away from previous themes to something that feels both modern and authoritative.

### "Trust & Prestige" Palette Proposal
*   **Primary Color (Brand):** Deep Royal Blue or Navy (e.g., `#1A365D` or `#0F4C81`). This matches his headshot, conveying trust, stability, and professionalism.
*   **Secondary/Accent Color:** Warm Champagne or Soft Gold (e.g., `#D4AF37` or `#F3E5AB`). This pairs beautifully with deep blue and adds a subtle touch of luxury.
*   **Background Base:** Soft Off-White (e.g., `#FAFAFA`) instead of stark white, with Light Slate for secondary sections.
*   **Text/Typography:** Charcoal or Slate Gray for high legibility without the harshness of pure black.

### Typography Adjustments
*   **Headings:** Introduce a sophisticated Serif font (such as *Playfair Display* or *Lora*) for primary headings (`h1`, `h2`) to give a classic, high-end real estate feel.
*   **Body Copy:** Keep a clean, modern Sans-Serif (such as *Inter* or *Outfit*) for perfect readability on all devices.

## 2. Homepage Image Integration (Hero Section)

Currently, the homepage hero section might just feature property images. To build immediate personal rapport, we can merge Tristan's headshot with the property imagery right as the user lands on the site.

### Idea A: The "Split Hero" Composition
*   **Layout:** A 50/50 or 40/60 split screen.
*   **Left Side:** Clean background featuring the main value proposition, headline, and calls-to-action (e.g., "Find Your Dream Home").
*   **Right Side:** A composite visual where a high-quality image of a beautiful home interior seamlessly fades or overlaps with Tristan's headshot (with background removed or creatively blended).
*   **Why it works:** It instantly connects the agent with the properties he represents.

### Idea B: The "Floating Glass Card" Overlay
*   **Background:** A full-width, stunning photograph of a Greater Sudbury property or landscape, perhaps slightly dimmed or blurred.
*   **Foreground:** A sleek, modern "glassmorphism" (frosted glass effect) card overlaid on one side of the hero image.
*   **Content:** Tristan's headshot elegantly breaks out of the top of this card, accompanied by a brief introductory welcome and a "Contact Me" button.
*   **Why it works:** It feels highly contemporary, premium, and keeps the initial focus on beautiful real estate while still introducing the agent contextually.

### Idea C: "Meet the Agent" Trust Banner
*   **Layout:** Immediately below a main property-focused hero slider, we introduce a full-width banner.
*   **Visual:** A panoramic collage. The left side features Tristan's portrait, extending into a gradient that reveals a backdrop of the local cityscape or a luxury property on the right.
*   **Why it works:** It allows the hero section to focus purely on the "dream home" aspect, while immediately following up with the strong personal branding required for trust.
